# /draft-proposal

**Usage:**
- `/draft-proposal [name] [company]` — pulls all context from CRM
- `/draft-proposal [name] [paste discovery call notes]` — uses notes provided

**What it does:** For Bizzed this is used when following up after
a demo or discovery call with a warm lead who needs a clear summary
of what Bizzed offers them specifically and what the next step is.
Not a traditional proposal — a personalised, deal-specific follow-up
document that converts interest into action.

---

## Instructions

### Step 1 — Pull the context
From CRM: name, role, what business they are acquiring, their timeline,
their main concern, what was shown in the demo, their funding situation.

Flag anything missing that would make this less personalised.

### Step 2 — Write the follow-up proposal

Use the tone-of-voice skill throughout.
Every section must reference their specific deal — not generic Bizzed features.

---

Subject: Bizzed — next steps for your [target business] acquisition

Hi [Name],

[Opening — one sentence referencing specifically what they are acquiring
and what stage they are at. Show you were listening.]

**What we looked at together**
[2 to 3 sentences summarising what was covered in the demo or call.
Reference the actual business they are acquiring if known.]

**What Bizzed gives you on this deal**
[3 bullet points — specific to their situation, not generic features.
e.g. "A complete financial projection from the P&L you shared"
not "Automated Financial Projections"]

**The analysis is free — here is how to start**
[Direct link or instruction to access the platform]
Start with the website URL: [their target's URL if known]
Or upload the P&L directly if you have it.

**On financing**
[One paragraph — only include if they raised financing in the conversation.
Reference their specific funding gap if discussed.]

**Security — because this matters**
Bizzed is ISO 27001 certified, GDPR compliant, and uses bank-level
encryption. Your deal data is strictly isolated — no one else sees it.

**Next step**
[One specific ask with a soft deadline.
e.g. "Start your free analysis this week and let's reconnect on
[specific date] to go through the results together."]

Best regards,
[Name]
Bizzed | bizzed.xyz

---

### Step 3 — Show for approval
Display the full draft.
Ask: "Shall I send this now, or would you like to make any changes first?"

### Step 4 — On approval
1. Send via Gmail to the contact's email address
2. Update CRM: log "Proposal sent" with date
3. Move deal to next stage
4. Set a 3-day follow-up reminder
5. Confirm: "Sent to [name] at [email]. Follow-up reminder set for [date]."
