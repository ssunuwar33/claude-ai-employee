# /summarise-thread

**Usage:**
- `/summarise-thread [paste the email thread]`
- `/summarise-thread [sender name]` — finds thread in Gmail automatically

**What it does:** Reads the full email thread, produces a sharp
summary of what was discussed, what was agreed, what is still
unresolved, and what needs to happen next — then offers to act.

---

## Instructions

### Step 1 — Read the full thread
Start from the very first email in the thread.
Read every message in chronological order.
Do not skim. Every message may contain important context about
the deal they are pursuing.

### Step 2 — Extract the key information

Who is involved:
Names, roles, and which side they are on — Bizzed or the lead.

What was discussed:
Key topics, questions they asked, information they shared,
details about the business they are acquiring.

What was agreed:
Any confirmed commitments from either side —
e.g. "they agreed to a demo on Thursday",
"they confirmed they are looking at a £500k acquisition".

What is still unresolved:
Questions that were not answered, decisions not yet made,
information they asked for that has not been sent.

What needs to happen next:
Specific actions, who owns each one, and by when.

### Step 3 — Produce the summary

---
THREAD SUMMARY — BIZZED
Between: [Names]
Date range: [First email date] to [Last email date]
Total emails: [X]

WHAT WAS DISCUSSED
- [Bullet — specific]
- [Bullet — specific]
- [Bullet — specific]

THEIR ACQUISITION TARGET
[What we know about the business they are looking to buy —
industry, size, price, location, any financials mentioned]

WHAT WAS AGREED
- [Confirmed commitments only — not maybes]

STILL UNRESOLVED
- [Open questions or pending decisions]

NEXT ACTIONS
[Action] — [Who owns it: Bizzed or them] — [Due: date or ASAP]
[Action] — [Who owns it] — [Due]
---

### Step 4 — Offer to act

"Would you like me to:
A) Draft a reply addressing the unresolved points
B) Send a summary email to them confirming what was agreed
C) Book the next meeting based on what was discussed
D) Log all of this in the CRM with a next action set

Reply with A, B, C, or D — or any combination."

### Step 5 — Execute and log
Whatever action is chosen — execute it fully.
Then log the thread outcome in the CRM:
- Key details about their acquisition target
- Current stage and sentiment
- Next action and due date
