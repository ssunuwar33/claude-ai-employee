## What we do

Bizzed transforms the business acquisition due diligence process using AI.
What traditionally takes weeks or months, Bizzed delivers in minutes.

A buyer can start with nothing more than a business website URL or a P&L
sheet and instantly receive a complete picture of the acquisition target.

## Our platform features

**Instant Insights**
Start with a website link or P&L sheet and get a complete insight
into the target business immediately. No lengthy setup required.

**Automated Financial Projections**
Instant financial analysis and forecasting from P&Ls or tax returns.
Removes the need for expensive accountants for initial analysis.

**Smart Due Diligence**
Clear picture of a business's potential and operational requirements
before the buyer commits to anything.

**Legal Document Generation**
Automatically generates all necessary legal documents for a business
acquisition. Over 400 legal docs already generated on the platform.

**Financing Options Analysis**
Explores and compares different financing options tailored to the
buyer's specific situation and the deal structure.

**Risk Assessment and Mitigation**
Identifies potential risks in the target business and provides
actionable mitigation strategies — not just a list of problems.

## Our ideal user

**Who they are:**
Business buyers, entrepreneurs, investors, and SME acquirers who are
actively looking to acquire a business or in the early stages of
evaluating one.

**Their situation:**
They have found a business they are interested in buying but the
traditional due diligence process is too slow, too expensive, or
too complex. They need fast, reliable insights without paying
£10,000+ in professional fees upfront.

**What triggers them to look for us:**
- They found a business for sale and want to move quickly
- They have been burned by a slow or expensive due diligence process before
- They are non-technical buyers who feel overwhelmed by financials
- They are repeat acquirers who want to speed up their deal flow

**Decision maker:** The buyer themselves — typically a founder,
entrepreneur, investor, or SME operator
